Our Story

A Sanctuary of Flavour

ملجأ النكهات الأصيلة

15
Years of
Excellence

Al-Waha — meaning The Oasis in Arabic — was born from a dream to bring the warmth, generosity and extraordinary cuisine of the Levant to the heart of the city. Like an oasis in the desert, we offer a place of refuge, beauty and nourishment.

Our chefs craft every dish from recipes passed down through generations, using the finest imported spices, slow-roasted meats and hand-rolled pastries. Every visit is a journey — from the mezze to the last spoonful of umm ali, we invite you to experience the soul of Arabic hospitality.

The décor draws from the golden light of desert evenings, with handcrafted lanterns, mosaic accents and the gentle sound of oud filling the air.
