Our Story

A Sanctuary of Flavour

ملجأ النكهات الأصيلة

15
Years of
Excellence

Al-Waha — meaning The Oasis in Arabic — was born from a vision to bring the warmth, generosity and extraordinary cuisine of the Levant to the heart of London. Like an oasis discovered in the vast desert, we offer a place of beauty, refuge and nourishment.

Our chefs craft each dish from recipes passed down through generations, using hand-selected spices, slow-roasted meats and pastries rolled with care. Every visit is a journey — from the first mezze to the final spoonful of umm ali.

The room draws from the golden light of desert evenings: hand-hammered lanterns, geometric tilework and the soft notes of oud drifting through the air.
